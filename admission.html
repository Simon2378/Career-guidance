Admission Requirements

HIGHER NATIONAL DIPLOMA (HND)

Duration: 2 years

  1. Photocopy of birth certificate
  2. Photocopy of ordinary level slip/certificate or its equivalent
  3. Photocopy of advanced slip/certificate
  4. Photocopy of BAC or its equivalent
  5. Photocopy of national ID card or passport (if applicable)
  6. Four (4) passport size photos
  7. At least a pass in any two (2) GCE advanced level papers (excluding Religion) - only for private institutes

Mentored by the University of Buea

BACHELOR'S DEGREES (BTECH/BSC)

Duration: 3 years

  1. GCE advanced level
  2. GCE ordinary level
  3. Photocopies of birth certificates and other certificates
  4. Passport size photographs (4) - NB: Coloured

Mentored by the University of Buea

BACHELOR'S DEGREE (TOP-UP BTECH/BSC)

Duration: 1 year

  1. Original HND/BTS (and photocopies)
  2. Photocopies of birth certificates and other certificates
  3. Four (4) passport size photographs (coloured)

Mentored by the University of Buea

MASTER'S DEGREE (MTECH/MBA/MSC)

Duration: 2 years

MBA/MSC Programs

  1. Original BSC certificate or attestation
  2. Photocopies of birth certificates
  3. Four (4) passport size photographs (coloured)

MTECH Program

  1. BTECH/DIPET II
  2. Photocopies of birth certificates and other certificates
  3. Four (4) passport size photographs (coloured)

Mentored by the University of Buea
